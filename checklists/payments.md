# Payments Checklist

- Stripe products and prices are configured in the correct mode.
- Checkout success and cancel URLs are set.
- Webhook endpoint is deployed and verified.
- Webhook signing secret is configured.
- Customer billing portal works.
- Paid access is enforced on the server.
- Trial, refund, cancellation, and failed payment behavior are defined.
- Test cards cover success, failure, and cancellation flows.
- Production keys are not used locally.
